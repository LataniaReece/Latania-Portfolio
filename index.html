AboutMe

Born and raised in Boston, Massachusetts with my huge family (8 chaotic yet lovable siblings, 2 amazing parents). I originally was pursuing a career in medicine, pediatric neurology, but frankly I wasn't happy and I was in it for the wrong reasons. After deciding to pass on a medical career, I was a bit lost. I had no idea what to do as a career....and then eventually I discovered coding.

Since my introduction to web development over two years ago, I've never been more determined to push myself. I finally found an engaging challenge that motivates me to consistently learn and develop my skills to create exceptional products. Not only has coding helped me find my ideal career choice but it's taught me that if I put my mind to it, I can honestly do anything. I've never been more passionate about a career and each day this passion grows.
